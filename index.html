Interactive Kiosk

Mack Truck Builder

A touch-screen kiosk built for museum floors — where real truck engineering becomes something kids and families can play with. Visitors configure every system and drive what they built. 60+ unique combinations, a real-time physics game loop, and a live leaderboard — built for the Da Vinci Science Center in Allentown, PA.

5-screen guided experience 60+ truck configurations 4 truck classes STEM content in 4 disciplines 60fps real-time physics Cloud leaderboard
More about this project

The experience moves Home → Factory → Customize → Drive → Leaderboard — five screens that walk visitors through the same decisions a real Mack engineer faces. Axle count. Wheelbase. Engine size. Fuel type. Tires. Aerodynamics. Accessories. Paint. Graphics.

Every choice feeds a scoring formula that rewards fuel efficiency. So the best-engineered truck literally earns more time on the road. That's not a metaphor. That's the mechanic.

The driving game is where STEM becomes visceral. Speed is tap-controlled — but each tap costs points. Kids feel the connection between smart engineering and real-world performance before they've ever heard the word "aerodynamics."

Deployed at the Da Vinci Science Center in Allentown, PA — the same city where Mack Trucks has built since 1905.

Team: 4O1! Creative

Native iOS App

SoBo

Built for the 36% of Americans who've lost a friendship over something borrowed. SoBo is a friendship-safe lending app that puts a friendly middleman between the lend and the ask — giving every loan its own negotiation thread, status tracking, and gentle nudge system. Lenders get their stuff back. Borrowers don't carry guilt. Built iOS-first on React Native and Supabase.

iOS + Google Play 5 loan statuses tracked end-to-end 2-sided invite loop AI humor baked into every nudge Auto reminders sent for you Discover nearby items
More about this project

The origin is specific: a $450 trailer hitch lent to a friend, forgotten, awkward to retrieve. SoBo was built from that moment — the invisible social tax that makes people quietly write off their stuff rather than deal with the ask. The app doesn't track items. It absorbs the awkwardness so the relationship stays intact.

Both lenders and borrowers move through a 5-status loan lifecycle — Pending, Active, Overdue, Returned, Canceled — with real-time conversation threads, before-and-after photos, and AI-timed nudges that land as friendly reminders, not debt collectors. The invite loop is structural: every loan creates a frictionless entry point for the borrower, turning each lend into organic distribution with zero ad spend.

A secondary Discover screen lets neighbors browse publicly listed items by location — building toward community lending networks over time. With a 3-phase monetization model (free tier → SoBo+ at $4.99/month → peer rental marketplace), the product is designed to grow from a single borrowed drill to a category-defining platform. Pre-seed raise in progress.
